# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

RubotPaul is a personal household assistant operated by Geoff Gallinger for use by
the operator's immediate household and a limited number of authorized contacts.
This policy describes what data the service collects, how it is used, and how
users can request its deletion.

## Who we are

- Operator: Geoff Gallinger
- Contact: rubotpaul.assistant@gmail.com
- Service: A self-hosted assistant accessible via Discord and SMS.

RubotPaul is not a commercial product and is not offered to the general public.

## What we collect

When you message RubotPaul via SMS, we receive and process:

- Your phone number
- The content of messages you send
- Timestamps and metadata associated with delivery
- For SMS verification of third-party services, the verification code contents

When you interact via Discord, we receive your Discord username, user ID, and
the content of messages you send to the service.

## How we use it

- To deliver responses to your messages
- To pass relevant context to large language model providers (Anthropic) for
  generating responses
- To maintain conversation history scoped to your individual user identity
- To deliver scheduled briefings and notifications you have requested

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or marketers. We do
not use your messages to train any model.

## Third parties involved

The service relies on the following providers, each governed by their own
privacy policies:

- **Twilio** — SMS delivery infrastructure
- **Anthropic** — language model API
- **Discord** — messaging platform
- **Hostinger** — server hosting
- **Google** (Calendar, Voice) — for calendar features and number management

## Retention

Message content and conversation history are retained for up to 90 days, after
which they are deleted from the operator's systems. SMS verification codes are
retained only as long as needed to complete the verification, typically minutes.

## Your rights

You can:

- Reply `STOP` to any SMS to opt out of all messages from this number
- Reply `HELP` to receive contact information
- Email rubotpaul.house@gmail.com to request a copy of your data or its deletion

We honor opt-out and deletion requests within 7 days.

## Security

The service runs on a private virtual server with restricted access, encrypted
SSH authentication, and outbound-only network connections to third-party APIs.
We do not store payment information; purchases are made through prepaid cards
with strict per-merchant and per-month spend limits.

## Children's privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
information from children. Household members under 13 may have their information
incidentally referenced by authorized adult users (e.g., a parent asking for a
schedule). Such references are treated as the personal data of the asking adult.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the
most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to active users via
their primary channel.
